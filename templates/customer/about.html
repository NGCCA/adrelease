Our Working process

Ad Release is a web application by which user can post advertisement of their company in their desirable ad agency

Online Ad Placement

our system will provide an online platform that allows advertisers to place ads from anywhere with an internet connection, eliminating the need for physical visits.

Transparent Pricing

Advertisers will have access to transparent pricing for various ad sizes and placements, making it easier to compare costs and make informed decisions.

Ad Agency Integration

Collaborate with various ad agencies to integrate their services into the platform, making it a one-stop destination for ad bookings.

The ultimate goal of the Ad Release project is to empower businesses to succeed in their advertising endeavors by providing a comprehensive platform that facilitates seamless connections between companies and ad agencies.

By offering a user-friendly interface, a wide range of advertising options, and effective tools for campaign management and analysis, Ad Release aims to streamline the advertising process and maximize the impact of each campaign. Ultimately, the project seeks to drive business growth, increase brand visibility, and foster long-term success for advertisers and ad agencies alike.

Creative Design

our system will include user-friendly ad creation tools, enabling advertisers to design and format their ads directly on the platform. Templates and multimedia options will be available

Online Payment

Secure online payment options will be integrated, allowing advertisers to make payments through credit/debit cards, digital wallets, or other online payment methods.

Broad Accessibility

The platform will offer a wide selection of ad agencies and newspapers, including local, regional, and national publications, providing advertisers with more choices.
